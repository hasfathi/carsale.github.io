Corporate Philosophy

With the highest of ethical standards & pure dedication to excellence all operational aspects, we aspire to exceed our customer expectations. We recognize one of the most challenging issues to date, that is trust & are uncompromising in our dedication to this integral element in all our business activities. We believe in a Trusted partnership, always. Looking to the future, we will continue to develop our already advanced enterprise & keep driving them onwards. We aim to consolidate a clear brand identity, product value & superior customer service. Despite an increasingly competitive business environment, we vow to create the added value that will deliver true satisfaction to our valued customers.




Source of Quality Vehicle Imports

We deal with a wide range of vehicles, comprising of all leading brands & models. We import used Japanese & European cars such as Honda fit hybrid,Honda grace,Honda insight,Honda vezel_rs,Suzuki spacia,Suzuki wagon R_Fx,Suzuki wagon R stingray,Toyota aqua,Toyota prius,Toyota axio. we specialize in machinery vehicles as well.. Crescent car sales are the main sole agents for Al Ain Japan (Pvt) Limited, which is one of the leading vehicle export companies in Japan. Al Ain Japan, which has own showrooms in almost 10 countries supplies quality vehicles to many other countries as well around the world. Al Ain Japan is known as the supplier of quality vehicles with a reliable service.




Wide Range of Vehicles

We deal with brand new & reconditioned vehicles. We import Japanese brands as well as European brands Hybrid cars on customer request. We have a holding stock of almost 300 vehicles comprising of 10 models of hybrid cars,machineries etc at any given time.




Services

Quality Assurance These vehicles go through a comprehensive quality check before it reaches the customers. We only get quality vehicles that undergo strict inspection to ensure that they meet the highest standard of quality. Japanese used cars go through stringent quality checks prior to exporting it to any other country. A unique grading system ensures the standard of the vehicles which helps the customers to determine the standard of the vehicle. Apart from this, all the vehicles we receive are closely checked by our expert automobile engineers to confirm the quality & standard before it's released for sales.
